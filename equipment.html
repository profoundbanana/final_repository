Shun Engetsu 8 inch Chef Knife:
The Shun Engetsu 8 inch Chef Knife is another in a collection of masterpieces by Shun. Baring the iconic logo that guarantees a product of the highest quality this beautiful knife boasts a Dual Core Steel blade that is engineered to stay sharp the longer you use it. It also comes in Shuns patented "D" shape handle making it by far the most comfortable knife to use for even extended periods of time. Trust me I know.

Kitchen Aid Stand Mixer:
The Kitchen Aid Artisan Stand mixer is not only an industry but an at-home gold standard. Nothing compares in durability and power and it truly is the mark of someone dedicated to the craft. At the reasonable price point of around 350 USD it may be a little steep for a beginner but a seasoned veteran knows that the quality simply can't be matched.

Vitamix A3500:
The Vitamix A3500 is an absolute workhorse. Vitamix is consistently outmatching it's competetors in just about every way and they truly know it. A blender, to many, is just a smoothie making tool but in the case of the Vitamix it is that and so much more but even if one were to only make smoothies the speed and quality of the Vitamix is matched only by outrageously expensive "Industrial-use" blenders. Whether it's soups, purees, aiolis, or even the humble smoothie, nothing will compare to the Vitamix.
