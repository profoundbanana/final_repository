Mission statement


The whole purpose of this site is to be a collection of recipes and equipment I endorse with articles written by "staff" about said recipes and equipment. Working as a chef was my passion for a very long time and now that the time has come to say goodbye to the chef life I figured this site would make a fun little tribute to both my time as a chef and to those still in the industry!
